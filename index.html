About me

I recently graduated from NITK Surathkal and I will soon be joining Microsoft Research India as a Research Fellow working with Dr. Akash Lal.
Before this, I worked with Dr. KC Sivaramakrishnan and Dr. Kartik Nagar of IIT Madras on verifying Mergeable Replicated Data Types based on functional data structures, using F* (See Peepul). Previously I also worked with Dr. KC Sivarmakrishnan on ParaFuzz, a fuzzer for Multicore OCaml programs.
My main interests lie in Distributed and Parallel Computing, Verification, Synthesis, and Optimization of Programs.
